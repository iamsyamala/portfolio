The Process

How I built it

01
🎨
Crafting the Brand Identity
"Create the brand identity including logo, archetype, persona, visual language."
Collaborated with a branding agency to define RideBuddy's core values, archetype (Primary: Caregiver / Secondary: Creator/Artist), and personality. Established the visual language across digital and physical touchpoints. Guiding philosophy: Humanizing Safe Mobility.
✅ Cohesive brand system ready for market
02
🏛️
Brand Positioning Through Events
"Showcase at industry events, generate leads, and position as thought leaders."
Led brand execution at Prawaas 4.0 (national mobility expo), JPMC CAO Innovation Summit, and a Bosch leadership soft launch. Designed modular event kiosks and managed full on-ground brand experience end to end.
✅ 2nd at JPMC Summit → 100-device pilot secured
03
🖥️
Developing Marketing Assets
"Develop all branding and marketing collaterals for pitches and campaigns."
Built ridebuddy.com, produced the product explainer video, created the user manual, directed the product photoshoot, and developed all supporting materials — standees, flags, packaging, merchandise, and car branding.
✅ Full asset library deployed across all pitches
04
📊
Strategic Tools for Growth
"Build the pitch deck for investor and corporate pitches."
Created the investor and corporate sales pitch deck used in engagements with Google, PwC, and JPMC. Developed visual and narrative assets that communicated RideBuddy's value proposition with clarity and conviction.
✅ Decks used in enterprise pitches across 3 majors
05
🚗
Everyday Brand Presence
"Create consistent brand presence across every real-world touchpoint."
Rolled out branded merchandise (apparel, cases, accessories), external car branding for the fleet, custom packaging, and in-cabin passenger-facing materials — creating a 360° brand experience at every interaction.
✅ Unified brand across digital + physical world
06
🎬
Event Videos & Storytelling
"Create video content that communicates product value at events."
Produced brand and product videos for events — including an animated explainer for the JPMC Summit and brand films for the stakeholder soft launch — ensuring every live showcase had compelling visual storytelling to back it up.
